Our Services

Pickup and Delivery Book Monitoring Feature

The Pickup and Delivery Book Monitoring feature provides a seamless way to manage and track laundry orders from start to finish. This system automates the scheduling, monitoring, and updating of both pickup and delivery bookings, ensuring that laundry service providers can efficiently coordinate with customers. With real-time status updates and detailed logs, it minimizes errors, enhances customer communication, and streamlines operations, making the entire process more organized and reliable. This ensures timely service while giving both laundry providers and customers full visibility over each transaction.

Digital Booking List Feature

The Digital Booking List Feature allows laundry service providers to efficiently manage and organize customer bookings in one centralized system. With this feature, all booking details, including customer information, service types, and schedules, are digitally recorded and accessible in real-time. This eliminates the need for manual entry, reduces the risk of scheduling conflicts, and streamlines operations. Users can easily view, edit, or update bookings, ensuring a more convenient and accurate process for both the service provider and the customer.

Digital Receipt Generator Feature

The Digital Receipt Generator Feature simplifies the billing process by automatically creating digital receipts for each completed transaction. After a service is rendered, the system generates a clear, professional receipt that includes all relevant details such as the service provided, total cost, and payment method. These receipts can be easily shared with customers via email or printed, reducing paperwork and providing a seamless, efficient experience for both the service provider and the customer.

Inventory Feature

The inventory feature allows laundry service providers to efficiently manage their supplies and equipment. It includes functionalities for tracking stock levels, monitoring usage, and generating alerts for low inventory. Users can categorize items, set reorder points, and view detailed reports on inventory status. This ensures that laundry operations run smoothly without interruptions due to supply shortages.

Expense Feature

The expense feature allows users to record, view, and manage all supply and utility expenses in one place. It provides an organized table of expense entries, supports adding new expenses, and enables easy tracking of spending for better financial management.

Report Feature

The report page provides a summary of sales, expenses, inventory, and key business insights for a selected period. Users can filter data, view trends through charts and tables, and export the report as a PDF. The layout is organized for quick reference and easy interpretation.
